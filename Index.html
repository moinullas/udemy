Ajith Kumar

The Thala of Tamil Cinema.

Ajith Kumar is an Indian film actor who works predominantly in Tamil cinema. In addition to his acting, he is also a motor car racer and participated in the MRF Racing series (2010). His other skills include cooking,[3][4] photography, air pistol shooting[5] and UAV/drone modelling
